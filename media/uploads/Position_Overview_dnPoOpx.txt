Data Scientist
Position Overview

We are seeking a motivated and skilled Mid-Level Data Scientist to join our team and help transform complex data into actionable insights. In this role, you will work on data-driven projects, develop predictive models, and contribute to building scalable data pipelines and infrastructures. If you are passionate about working with data and continuously expanding your skill set, we’d love to hear from you!

Responsibilities:

• Identify and source relevant data to address business needs.

• Collect, process, and clean structured and unstructured data from various sources.

• Build and maintain predictive models and machine learning algorithms to solve complex business challenges.

• Enhance data collection processes to optimize data quality and usability.

• Develop and optimize data pipelines, architectures, and datasets.

• Perform exploratory data analysis to identify trends, patterns, and insights.

• Generate visualizations and reports using Power BI or similar BI tools to communicate findings effectively.

• Work with large datasets using Python and SQL for analysis and modeling.

• Maintain and improve databases, ensuring data quality and accessibility.

• Collaborate with cross-functional teams to implement data-driven solutions.

Requirements:

• Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science, Mathematics, or a related technical field.

• 3+ years of experience in data science or a related role.

• Proficiency in Python and SQL for data analysis, modeling, and pipeline development.

• Experience with Power BI or similar BI platforms for data visualization.

• Hands-on experience analyzing large datasets and applying machine learning algorithms.

• Strong understanding of data infrastructure and pipeline development.

• Proven ability to process, cleanse, and verify data integrity.

• A keen eye for identifying trends and generating actionable insights.

• Strong interest in solving data management challenges and a commitment to continuous learning.

• English level: B2 or higher for effective communication in a distributed team.

Nice to Have:

• Experience with cloud platforms (e.g., AWS, Azure, or Google Cloud).

• Familiarity with advanced machine learning frameworks (e.g., TensorFlow, PyTorch, or

Scikit-learn).

• Knowledge of data governance practices.

• Previous experience in building and optimizing databases or data warehouses.

What We Offer:

• A dynamic and supportive environment focused on personal and professional growth.

• Opportunities to work on innovative projects and implement cutting-edge technologies.

• Flexible working hours and a remote-friendly culture.

• Access to training resources and tools to enhance your skills.
